# postoperative-pain-detection

This research focuses on the experimental development of a Black Phosphorus–Zinc Oxide (BP-ZnO) nanorod composite biosensor for rapid screening of inflammatory biomarkers associated with postoperative pain. Black phosphorus provides excellent electrical conductivity and biomolecule interaction, while ZnO nanorods offer high surface area, biocompatibility, and improved electron transport. The BP-ZnO composite sensor is fabricated experimentally using hydrothermal synthesis and liquid exfoliation techniques.
